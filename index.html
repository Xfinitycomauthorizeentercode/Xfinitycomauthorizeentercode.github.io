Xfinity.com/authorize

Xfinity.com/authorize is where you activate the Xfinity Stream app by entering an activation code.



The Xfinity Stream app allows users to stream live TV, on-demand shows, and movies on various devices. It offers access to Xfinity's content library and DVR recordings remotely. To activate, visit Xfinity.com/authorize, enter the activation code, and follow the prompts to start enjoying your Xfinity subscription on your preferred device.

Sign up for Xfinity Authorize Account

  1. Go to Xfinity.com/authorize.
  2. Click on the tab - "Create xfinity account".
  3. Provide username, email id, password, etc.
  4. Click on "Create account".
  5. Once the xfinity account is created successfully, sign in using credentials.

How to Download and Install Xfinity App?

  1. Visit the respective app store of your iOS device or Android device.
  2. Use the search bar to find the official Xfinity app.
  3. Once you find the Xfinity app, tap on it, then tap the "Download" or "Install" button.

Activate Xfinity Stream app : Xfinity.com/authorize

  1. Launch the Xfinity Stream app on your device.
  2. Log in to your Xfinity account using your username and password.
  3. Follow any prompts in the app to enter the activation code shown on your screen.
  4. Enter the code and follow any additional on-screen instructions to complete the activation process.

The Xfinity app allows you to manage your Xfinity services, pay bills, troubleshoot, and more, depending on your Xfinity.com/authorize subscription and services.
